    Men's Tuxedo Shirts Are a Classic for Formal Attire

    Every man should own a version of the men's tuxedo shirt for those special occasions when formal wear is required. These shirts are available in several styles and even colors, for a more unique look, such as bright or bold hues to match wedding schemes.

    Collar styles of men's tuxedo shirts

    One way to begin your search for the best-fitting tuxedo shirt is to find the collar style that appeals to you. There are three basic styles to wear with the classic tux, including the wing collar, laydown collar, and banded collar.

    Wing collar

    The most formal of collars, you typically accessorize the wing tip with a bow tie, which you place in front of the tips for a pulled-together look. This collar pairs well with a full-back vest or cummerbund.

    Laydown collar

    For a more casual, relaxed look, the laydown, or turndown, collar is an appropriate choice that you can wear with a bow tie or classic men's tie. This is a suitable option if you fancy a shirt in a nontraditional color like pink or mint.

    Banded collar

    If you're not a huge fan of wearing ties, consider the banded collar tuxedo shirt. Whether Mandarin or Victorian style, this is a fashion-forward look that bends the rules but is still within the realm of good taste to wear with a tuxedo.
